Instructions

add domain users (distribute IT student names to all dept/section/area) I. there are 4 academic departments 1. Basic Art and Sciences Department a. BTVTE Electrical b. BTVTE Electronics c. BTVTE Computer Hardware a. BTVTE Computer Software 2. Electrical and Allied Department a. information technology b. electrical engineering c. electronics engineering d. instrumentation and control technology e. BET Mechatronics 3. Mechanical and Allied Department a. mechanical engineering technology b. rac engineering technology c. non destructive engineering technology d. electromechanical engineering technology e. automotive engineering technology f. BET ElectroMechanical 4. Civil and Allied Department a. civil engineering b. civil engineering technology c. environmental science d. chemical engineering technology II. administrative department consists of the following: 1. registrar's office 2. director's office 3. research and extension 4. human resource and management office 5. finance office 6. supply and procurement 7. records office 8. library services III. create domain users for each department/office and technology section. use their real names when creating the domain account IV. create an organizational unit for each technology section or office (i.e. computer engineering, electronics, registrar, research etc.) V. all users can only log on to workstations (xp class a networl, 7 class b network) that were assigned to their respective departments/offices VI. all users should change their passwords every 30 days VII. academic users can log on from 7:30 am - 8:45am while administrative staff users can log on from 7:00am - 7:00pm
